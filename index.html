Reclaim your attention.

We all feel it: our relationship with technology has become unhealthy and unsustainable. Social media should help us connect — not serve corporate interests. Pixeldrop is a small rebellion for people curious about a healthier way. It’s for those who want to take back their attention yet still believe technology can be good when it serves human connection.

Ethical design

built for human connection, not doomscrolling

Weekly feed refresh

Your feed updates once a week, creating space to live first and share later.

No algorithmically served content

Only posts from people you follow. No explore page. No injected profiles. No endless “for you.”

No performative metrics

We remove public like and follower counts to dial down comparison and clout-chasing.

Tight privacy controls

Private-by-default options, clear account controls, and notifications that don’t hijack your attention.

What we stand for

Pixeldrop is a different kind of social app.

No ads. No tracking.

We don’t sell attention or collect shadow profiles. No ad-tech, no tracking pixels, no third-party data brokers.

Federated with ActivityPub

Pixeldrop is part of the Fediverse: communities can run their own servers and still follow each other across the network.

Open source

Our code is open and forkable. We build in public so anyone can inspect, contribute, or run their own instance.

Made to be created with

We encourage people to create their own spaces, experiment, and contribute — not lock them in.

Not for sale

We’re aiming for a social network free from corporate capture. Community support keeps the lights on; governance keeps incentives aligned.

Choice and control

You choose who you follow, and each server chooses what to accept. We default to media-first timelines and clear, local moderation.

Join the alpha

No spam, no trackers. We’ll email you when there’s something to try.
