Design features

Built for less doomscrolling and more delight.

Weekly feed refresh

Your feed updates once a week, creating space to live first and share later.

No algorithmically served content

Only see posts from people you follow. No explore page. No injected profiles. No endless “for you.”

No performative metrics

We remove public like and follower counts to dial down comparison and clout-chasing.

Tight privacy controls

Private by default options, account-level controls, and thoughtful notifications that don’t hijack your attention.

What we stand for

Pixeldrop is a different kind of social app.

No ads. No tracking.

We don’t sell attention or collect shadow profiles. There’s no ad-tech, pixels, or third‑party trackers on this site or in the app.

Federated with ActivityPub

Pixeldrop is part of the Fediverse. That means communities can run their own servers and still follow each other across the network.

Open source

Our code is open and forkable. We build in public so anyone can inspect, contribute, or run their own instance.

Made to be created with

We encourage people to create their own spaces, experiment, and contribute — not lock them in.

Not for sale

We’re aiming for a social network free from corporate capture. Community support keeps the lights on; governance keeps incentives aligned.

Choice and control

You choose who you follow, and each server chooses what to accept. We default to media-first timelines and clear, local moderation.
