When design harms mental health

Instagram’s attention-driven design can amplify anxiety, shorten attention spans, and reduce in-person connection.

  • Endless feeds: encourage compulsive scrolling linked to lower wellbeing.[1]
  • Public metrics: fuel social comparison and anxiety.[2]
  • Algorithmic amplification: boosts sensational posts that keep us hooked.[3]
